# GVSwift — Shipping Policy

**Effective Date:** 16 July 2026  
**Last Updated:** 16 July 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

---

## 1. Delivery Area

GVSwift currently delivers **within Andhra Pradesh only**. Delivery is limited to serviceable pincodes. Serviceability is checked in real-time at checkout — if your pincode is not serviceable, you will be notified before placing the order.

---

## 2. Payment Method

All orders are **Cash-on-Delivery (COD) only**. Payment is collected in cash at the time of delivery. The maximum order value for COD is **₹10,000**.

---

## 3. Shipping Charges

| Order Value | Shipping Charge |
|---|---|
| All orders (current launch) | ₹0 (Free shipping) |

Shipping charges are subject to change. Any applicable charges will be clearly displayed in the order summary before you confirm your order.

---

## 4. Delivery Timelines

| Zone | Estimated Delivery |
|---|---|
| Visakhapatnam city | 1–2 business days |
| Other Andhra Pradesh districts | 3–5 business days |

These are estimates only. Actual delivery may vary due to:

- Public holidays and local events.
- Weather conditions or natural events.
- High order volume periods.
- Courier availability in remote pincodes.

GVSwift does not guarantee delivery within the estimated timeframe and is not liable for delays beyond our reasonable control.

---

## 5. Order Processing

- Orders are processed on business days (Monday–Saturday, excluding public holidays).
- Orders placed after [CUTOFF TIME, e.g., 2 PM] may be processed the next business day.
- You will receive an email once your order is confirmed and another when it is shipped.

---

## 6. Delivery Attempts

- Our delivery partner will attempt delivery up to **2 times**.
- If delivery fails on both attempts (e.g., no one available at the address, incorrect address, customer unavailable), the order will be marked **Return-to-Origin (RTO)** and your account will be noted.
- Repeated failed deliveries may result in COD restrictions on your account.

---

## 7. Tracking

- Order status is updated in real-time in your **My Orders** page.
- Status stages: PLACED → CONFIRMED → SHIPPED → OUT_FOR_DELIVERY → DELIVERED.
- At MVP launch, tracking is status-based only. Courier tracking numbers may not be available until Shiprocket integration is completed (post-MVP).

---

## 8. Incorrect Address

- You are responsible for providing an accurate and complete delivery address.
- GVSwift is not liable for non-delivery due to an incorrect or incomplete address provided by you.
- Address changes after an order is CONFIRMED may not be possible. Contact support immediately at [SUPPORT EMAIL] if you need to change your address.

---

## 9. Contact

For shipping queries, raise a support ticket via the Platform or email [SUPPORT EMAIL].

---

*End of Shipping Policy.*
