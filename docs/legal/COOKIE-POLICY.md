# GVSwift — Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** 16 July 2026  
**Last Updated:** 16 July 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

---

## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. GVSwift uses cookies and similar technologies to operate the Platform and, with your consent, to analyse usage.

---

## 2. Cookies We Use

### 2.1 Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are required for the Platform to function. They cannot be disabled.

| Cookie / Technology | Provider | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Supabase Auth session token | Supabase | Keeps you logged in across pages |
| CSRF token | GVSwift (Next.js) | Prevents cross-site request forgery on form submissions |
| Cookie consent preference | GVSwift | Remembers your consent choice so we don't ask every page |

### 2.2 Analytics Cookies (Consent Required)

These cookies are only set **after you explicitly accept** via the cookie consent banner. You can withdraw consent at any time.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics 4 | Distinguishes unique users | 2 years |
| `_ga_[ID]` | Google Analytics 4 | Session persistence | 2 years |

GA4 is configured to **anonymise IP addresses** and events are only sent after consent. No behavioural data is sent to Google before you accept cookies.

---

## 3. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

- A cookie consent banner is shown on your first visit.
- You can accept or decline analytics cookies.
- You can change your preference at any time via the **Cookie Settings** link in the footer.
- Withdrawing analytics consent will stop GA4 tracking immediately. Essential cookies will remain active.

---

## 4. Third-Party Cookies

We do not allow third-party advertising or tracking cookies on GVSwift. The only third-party cookie used is Google Analytics 4, which is consent-gated as described above.

---

## 5. Contact

For cookie-related queries, email support@gvswift.com.
