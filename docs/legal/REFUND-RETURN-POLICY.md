# GVSwift — Refund and Return Policy

**Effective Date:** 16 July 2026  
**Last Updated:** 16 July 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

---

## 1. Return Window

You may request a return within **7 calendar days of the delivery date** as recorded in our system. Requests after 7 days will not be accepted.

---

## 2. Eligible Return Conditions

A product is eligible for return if:

- It is in **unused, unwashed condition** with all original tags attached.
- It is in its **original packaging** (box, wrapper, accessories if any).
- The return request is raised within the 7-day window via "My Orders" → "Request Return".
- The item is not in the non-returnable category listed in Section 5.

---

## 3. How to Initiate a Return

1. Log in to your GVSwift account.
2. Go to **My Orders** and open the delivered order.
3. Click **Request Return** and select a reason.
4. Submit the request. Our team will review it within 2 business days.
5. If approved, we will arrange self-pickup from your delivery address at no charge to you.
6. Once the item is received and inspected by us, we will confirm acceptance.

---

## 4. Refunds

Since GVSwift currently operates on **Cash-on-Delivery (COD) only**, no money is collected from you until delivery. As a result:

- For COD orders: there is no monetary refund. The return is accepted and the transaction is closed.
- If a prepaid payment option is introduced in the future, refunds for eligible returns will be processed to the original payment method within **7–10 business days**.
- Any store credit or alternative compensation is at GVSwift's sole discretion.

---

## 5. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned under any circumstances:

- Innerwear, socks, or any intimate apparel.
- Perishable goods.
- Customised or personalised items.
- Items marked "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" on the product page.
- Items returned without original packaging, tags, or accessories.
- Items showing signs of use, damage, or tampering not caused by us.

---

## 6. Damaged or Wrong Items

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item:

- Report it within **48 hours of delivery** by creating a support ticket via My Orders → Report an Issue.
- Attach photos clearly showing the issue.
- We will arrange a replacement or return at our cost after verification.
- Damage caused by misuse, accidental damage, or normal wear is not covered.

---

## 7. Order Cancellation Refunds

- If you cancel an order before it is shipped, the order is cancelled and no money changes hands (since payment is on delivery).
- If we cancel an order due to stock unavailability or risk flags, you will be notified and the order will be removed from your history.

---

## 8. Contact

For any return or refund queries, raise a support ticket via the Platform or email [SUPPORT EMAIL].

---

*End of Refund and Return Policy.*
